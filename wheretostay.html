Where To Stay

Our home base for transportation is a town called Varenna, which is super easy from a tourist perspective and for getting to the venue. See below for some options for lodging, in order of ease:

  • Rent one of the many VRBOs or AirBnBs in Varenna!
    • Sample search for 4 in Varenna on AirBnB
    • Sample search for 4 in Varenna on VRBO
    Look at hotels in Varenna! Some of the options are laid out below.
  • Look at Perledo - it's in the hills above Varenna and is beautiful, but it's quite a hill. There are a lot of amazing VRBOs / AirBnBs up there (and it’s where we stayed when we were there!, but please do know it's a 20-30 minute climb when you go in / out of town (or a 5-10 min cab if you're so inclined). If there's critical mass up in these parts, we'll send some buses up there too for the event.
  • We reserved a large number of rooms in advance at some of the following places as backups in case our guests can't find anything: Hotel Royal Victoria, Hotel Montecodeno, Hotel Olivedo, and Villa Central Varenna.
  • If you strike out in that area, let us know and we can figure out other options -- there are tons of options in Bellagio and Lecco, which are just a bit more inconvenient but doable

Varenna Hotels Overview

Hotel Olivedo
  • Right by the ferry port
  • Easy walking distance to station, shops, bars, restaurant and boats
Hotel Royal Victoria
  • On the main piazza
  • Easy walking distance to station, shops, bars, restaurant and boats
  • 10/15 minutes walk from station
  • Swimming pool
Albergo del Sole
  • On the main piazza
  • Easy walking distance from bars, shops, restaurants and boats
  • 10 – 15 minutes walk from station
Hotel Montecodeno
  • Close to the ferry port
  • Easy walking distance from station, bars, shops, restaurants and boats
Albergo Milano
  • Hotel & self catering apartments
  • Fairly close to the ferry port
  • Easy walking distance from bars, shops and restaurants. 10 minutes walk from station
Hotel Du Lac
  • Wonderful central location
  • Easy walking distance from bars, shops and restaurants. 10 minutes walk from station
  • Amazing views of Lake Como
Villa Monti
  • Villa divided into holiday apartments
  • Easy walking distance from bars, shops and restaurants. 15 minutes walk from station (including a hill)
  • From its balconies and windows it offers a 360° view of Varenna and Lake Como
  • Swimming pool and sun terrace overlooking the lake
